About AquaFlow

Providing innovative water solutions for Thika residents with cutting-edge technology and reliable service.

Revolutionizing Water Management in Thika

AquaFlow is a comprehensive water billing and management system designed specifically for the needs of Thika residents. Our platform integrates seamlessly with M-Pesa to provide convenient payment options and real-time water usage tracking.

With AquaFlow, residents can monitor their water consumption, pay bills instantly, and report issues directly through our platform. Administrators benefit from powerful analytics, automated billing, and efficient customer management.

Our mission is to make water management transparent, efficient, and accessible for everyone in Thika.
